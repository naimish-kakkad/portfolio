Philosophy & Approach

Bridging Strategy
and Execution

Payment architecture is not a technology problem — it is a business design problem. I work at that intersection, translating complex regulatory, operational, and financial requirements into systems that are resilient, auditable, and built to evolve.

Over 13 years I have worked across the full stack of financial infrastructure: from ISO 8583 message parsing at the network edge to orchestrating cloud-native core banking migrations on Mambu and FLEXCUBE. I partner with CXOs to align architecture decisions with business outcomes, and with engineering leaders to raise the technical bar.

My approach is practitioner-first — every architectural recommendation is grounded in operational reality, regulatory risk, and total cost of ownership. I do not design for whiteboards; I design for production.
